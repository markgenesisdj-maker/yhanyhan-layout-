About Yhanyhan RTW Shop

Founded in 2022 by Eljohn and Mae Balajadia, Yhanyhan RTW Shop is located at Taytay Tiangge, Taytay, Rizal. We specialize in ready-to-wear dresses and ternos for young girls aged 5 to 10 years old, featuring original designs created by the owners themselves. They have their own dressmaking shop or garments shop where every piece is carefully cut, trimmed, and sewn to ensure quality and style. Located at 731 Kasinay Street, Darangan, Binangonan, Rizal

Since our establishment, we've grown to offer over 50 different designs of kids' dresses and ternos - all crafted to be stylish, affordable, and made with quality materials that parents can trust.

We're now expanding our reach online to make it easier for more customers to discover and shop our collection from anywhere.

To empower and support local designers by providing stylish, comfortable, and high-quality ready-to-wear dresses and ternos for girls aged 5-10 years old, to showcase original designs, and to strengthen the shop's presence.
