About Yhanyhan RTW Shop

Founded in 2022 by Eljohn and Mae Balajadia, Yhanyhan RTW Shop is located at Taytay Tiangge, Taytay, Rizal. We specialize in ready-to-wear dresses and ternos for young girls aged 5 to 10 years old, featuring original designs created by the owners themselves.

Since our establishment, we've grown to offer over 50 different designs of kids' dresses and ternos — all crafted to be stylish, affordable, and made with quality materials that parents can trust.

We're now expanding our reach online to make it easier for more customers to discover and shop our collection from anywhere.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading and most trusted RTW shops in Taytay Rizal Tiangge, Known for original, high-quality. and affordable dresses - while continously expanding our market reach

Our Mission

To empower and support local designers by providing stylish, comfortable, and high-quality ready-to-wear dresses and ternos for girls aged 5-10 years old, to showcase original designs, and to strengthen the shop's presence.

Our Goals

  • Release at least 100 unique designs of kid's dresses and ternos within one year.
  • Reach a wider market by promoting high-quality, stylish kids wear to more customer
  • Continously improve product quality and design to build trust and encourage repeat purchases.
